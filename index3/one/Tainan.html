▴ Visiting Qigu Salt Mountain, its white salt mountain peak is approximately six stories tall, making it a spectacle to behold
▴ Visiting Black-faced Sooonbill Ecology Exhibition Hall, black-faced spoonbills will arrive in the protection area whenever winter, you can get a closer view to these beautiful birds
▴ Visiting Qigu Lagoon, we will take recreational fishing rafts across the lagoon, you can see egrets and oyster sheds in this voyage
▴ You will arrive on an isolated offshore sandbank called "Wanzailiao Barrier Land", where you can explore the uninhabited island and gaze into the magnificent Taiwan Strait
▴ After this voyage, you can have roast oyster for all-you-can-eat
