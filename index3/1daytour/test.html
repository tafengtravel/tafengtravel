▴ Visiting Taroko Archway, this is the first site visitors will encounter when entering Taroko National Park

▴ Having the remarkable Taiwanese indigenous style lunch in Taroko Village Hotel
▴ Visiting Swallow Grotto Trail ,The best place to view the Taroko Gorge inside the national park
The walls on each side of the river contain caves which form natural nesting places for spring swallow birds, bringing life to the area nearby the roaring river, with locals naming it Swallow Grotto
▴ Visitin Jiuqudong Trail, one of the most scenic sections of Taroko Gorge
The narrow Gorge with its steep sides close to each other presents a thrilling, majestic landscape
▴ Visiting Changchun Shrine, the commemoration of 226 people who died during the construction of the Central Cross-Island Highway
